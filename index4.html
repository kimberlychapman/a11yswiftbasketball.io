Player Fouls

Player fouls are when a player makes contact with another player in a way that breaks the rules.

This challenge focuses on 2 Types of Player Fouls:



All players begin the game with a personal foul count of 0.

Each time a player gets a personal foul called on them, 1 foul is added to their foul count. After the player gets a consequence for the foul, they can keep playing.
If a player gets to 6 personal fouls, they get "ejected"— kicked out of the game.

Flagrant 2 Fouls don't follow this same math. In a Flagrant 2 Foul, the foul count does not matter.

If a player gets a Flagrant 2 Foul called on them, they are automatically ejected from the game. A Flagrant Foul 2 is so serious that the player automatically gets kicked out of the game.
